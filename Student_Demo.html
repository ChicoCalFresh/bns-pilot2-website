Student Demographics

The following set of questions were asked in regards to the students academic demographic. This includes information such as what Cal State University they are currently enrolled in, their class standing, major, GPA, and enrolled units. They were also asked questions about programs they participate in as well as what type of student they are (transfer, international, etc.) to help identify groups of students.

School

Which Cal State University are you currently enrolled in? (n=415, 100% of 415 reporting). Three schools participated in this pilot of the survey: California State University, Chico (151), California State University, Fresno (138), and CCalifornia State University, Northridge (126). From the above graph, it seems that students from each campus will be nearly equally represented in the data.

Class Standing

Estimate your class standing by units completed (n=395, 95% of 415 reporting). It seems that the majority of the people are either in their Junior (135 people) or Senior (247 people) semester. There are a few Sophomores and very few Freshman or higher education students.

Major

What is your major? (n=394, 95% of 415 reporting). The majority of students in the data seem to be majoring in some type of Public Health, Social Services, Social Science, Education program (160 people). 108 of the students are Science, Technology, Engineering and Math (STEM) majors. Students were also given the option of “Other” to fill in a major if it did not fall into one of the above categories and of the 38 who selected this, 28 gave their responses below.

Overall GPA

What is your approximate overall grade point average (GPA)? (n=397, 96% of 415 reporting). The most common GPA for the data ranges between 3.01-3.5 (159 students), followed by3.51 or above (105 students). There are also 97 students who have an overall GPA between 2.51-3.0.

Currently Enrolled Units

How many units are you currently taking? (n=393, 95% of 415 reporting). Note that “22+” was rounded for 22 in the following analysis for the currently enrolled number of units.

The average number of units that a student in this data is enrolled in is 13.65 units, while the median is 14 units. It seems that the majority of students are considered full-time (12 or more units), with 12 units being most common and 15 units following that.
